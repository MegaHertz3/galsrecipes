Ingredients | Serves 1 | 5 minutes
Juice from 1 lemon
1½ teaspoons grated fresh ginger
1 teaspoon tumeric
¼ teaspoon black pepper
1 small garlic clove, grated with a Microplane
1 bag of green tea
Optional: 1 teaspoon honey

Instructions
Place all the ingredients into a mug and fill with nearly boiling water
Let the tea brew for 2-3 minutes then remove the bag and sip away
Tip: leave a spoon in the mug and give it a stir before each sip to mix up the spices.
