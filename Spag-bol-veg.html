Ingredients | Serves 3 | 15 minutes
1 Carrot, chopped
1 Celery stick sliced
1 Onion diced
handful of mushrooms diced
1 can tomato soup
1 can chopped tomatoes
Large dollop tomatoe puree
1 chrushed garlic clove
Quorn or Tesco veg mincemeat
spaghetti - wholemeal
salt & pepper
Oregano

Instructions
In a large saute pan , on medium heat, cook the onions in the olive oil (optional - add tumeric)
Add mushrooms, carrots, celery, garlic, cook unitl soften, ~ 5minutes
Add veg mincemeat, cook for another ~ 5 minutes
Add tomato soup, chopped tomatoes, and puree, salt, pepper and oregano
simmer for ~~ 15-30 minutes, until all nice an squidgy
prepare spaghetti
