Ingredients | Serves 4 | 1 hour
225g uncooked pasta
2 courgettes
2 red onions sliced
2 garlic cloves crushed
Olive oil/td>
1 red chili sliced (optional)
Optional veg -1 red/yellow/green pepper, mushrooms
600g tomato passata
Basil (optional Oregano)
sea salt and pepper
optional - grated cheese

Instructions
Preheat the oven to 190°C/375°F/gas 5
cook pasta and drain
fry or griddle or bake vegatables with garlic
add to pan if not already frying, then add chili, basil, and tomatoe passata, bring to a gentle simmer
stir in pasta, and season with salt and pepper
transfer to ovenproof dish, and bake for 30mins (optional - after 10mins add grated cheese on top) return to oven and continue to bake for 20mins
